📋 How It Works
Who can join?
Rising seniors (current Class of 2027) are eligible to apply for all five committees.
How often do we meet?
Once a month during a scheduled period with Student Life staff.
What's the commitment?
Attend monthly meetings, work on committee projects, and present to administration when needed.
What do I get?
Leadership experience, a line on college applications, and the chance to actually change things at OBHS.
🏆 What Is Battle of the Classes?

Battle of the Classes is one of the most exciting traditions at OBHS — a competition where each grade goes head-to-head in athletic events and performs a dance. Whether you're competing or cheering, everyone plays a role.

Sign up & pay your $10 to lock in your spot
After signing up, pay your Class Advisor $10. This covers your t-shirt, which is also your ticket to the event.
